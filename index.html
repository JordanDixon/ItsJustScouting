Twins Mavery and Adele were the first two girls to join Cub Scouts in Marietta, Ohio.

Although Mavery and Adele were the first girls to join Cub Scouts Pack 207 of Marrieta, Ohio, to them it was as simple as making them official participants of a club they had been around for all their lives.

Boy Scouts of America has made the decision to officially allow girls to join the Cub Scout program in a new recruitment effort called, "Scout Me In."

Cub Scouts is a family-based program for elementary school age children. Each grade level has a rank and is associated with an animal or symbol for fourth and fifth graders. The Ranks meet by dens during the meetings, which are split by gender, coming together for activities and events.

Cub Scout Rankings

Cub Scouts is divided into rankings. These rankings are determined by elementary grade level. Mavery and Adele are Webelos, the last ranking before they are allowed to join Boy Scouts next year.

Bobcat

The first rank earned by every boy in Cub Scouting. No matter age or grade a youth joins Cub Scouting, the youth must earn his Bobcat badge before the youth can be awarded any other rank.

Tiger

A child who is in the first grade (or is 7 years old).

Wolf

The rank designed for a second-grade or 8-year-old Cub Scout.

Bear

The rank designed for a Cub Scout in the third grade (or 9 years old).

Webelo

(Pronounced WEE-buh-los.)
A Cub Scout who has completed the third grade or is age 10 but has not yet completed fifth grade or reached age 11 1/2, and is a member of a Webelos den.

MAVERY AND Adele were the first two girls to attend Seven Ranges Scout Reservation on the Webelo Resident Camp Program, which is for boys and girls who are transitioning from Cub Scouts to Scouts BSA. Seven Ranges has been operated under the Buckeye Council since 1987 and each summer over 5,000 campers gather in Kensington, Ohio.
Older Scouts earn work towards merit badges while at the Webelo Resident Camp the girls and other Webelos earn their colors and work towards their arrow of light, which is the only badge a cub scout can carry over to Scouts BSA.

The Webelo Colors are optional yellow, red, and green emblem onto which the Webelos Activity Badges and Arrow of Light Adventure Pins may be pinned. It is worn on the right sleeve immediately below and touching the U.S. flag. Mavery and Adele last summer earn their Craftsman badge and the Aquanaut badge.
